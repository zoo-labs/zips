## Abstract

This ZIP establishes the foundational infrastructure for the Zoo decentralized AI research network.

## Motivation

Open science requires decentralized infrastructure that resists censorship and enables global collaboration.

## Specification

### Network Architecture

1. **Research Nodes**: Compute providers
2. **Storage Layer**: IPFS-based data storage
3. **Coordination Layer**: Research project management
4. **Incentive Layer**: Token-based rewards

### Governance

DAO-based governance with researcher voting rights.

## Security Considerations

Cryptographic verification of all research artifacts.
